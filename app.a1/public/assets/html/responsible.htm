USER RESPONSIBLE GAMING POLICY

The central goal of the User Responsible Gaming Policy is universally to ensure that Users of this Application safely and responsibly enjoy the game(s) as a form of entertainment, fun, and leisure.

It aims to secure for each Application User the control all the time of his or her gaming activities, preventing compulsiveness, gaming disorder, and becoming a problem gamer/gambler that will sow discord in the personal and family life of the User.

A Problem Gamer/Gambler is an individual affected by gaming or gambling to the point of no longer having normal control or rational judgment, leading to problems in other areas of his or her life.

Since the Application is catering to an online, non-physical gaming set-up, the Policy will engender upon the User the parameters to which he or she shall institute upon himself or herself to keep in tow with being a responsible gamer or User of the Application for his or her own benefit.

Age Restriction:

In consonance with existing laws and policies of gaming authorities, those aged twenty-one (21) years old and below are strictly restricted from using the Application.

Place Restriction:

User is highly encouraged not to access the Application and play the games while in religious, educational, and work places, areas, or premises.

Self-Restriction on Alcoholic Beverages:

User shall not allow himself or herself to play when he or she has consumed alcoholic beverages, especially when intoxicated.

Absolutely Drug-Free Gaming:

User must always ensure a Drug-Free environment not only in using the Application but for all time.

Self-Assessment on Game Spending:

User is encouraged to assess himself or herself as to his or her game spending in ensuring that he or she only bet or wager using money at the very least in excess of the personal and family needs.

Spending Extension Self-Restriction:

User shall desist from extending credit to any minor and problem gamer/gambler.

Self-Assessment of Gaming/Gambling Problematic Situations/Conditions and Self- Imposed Restriction(s):

User is encouraged to continuously assess himself or herself as to his or her emotional and financial status which might bear towards problematic situation or condition resulting to becoming problem gamer/gambler in accordance with the definition herein provided. In this way, User shall impose self-restrictions that will prevent or arrest the situation and evaluate from time to time to engage in the game in terms of progression.

Circumstances That Should Discourage User From Engaging in Gaming:

If the User is suffering from depression, is sad, and feeling down, she is encourage not to engage in the game(s) of the Application.

Help Line:

A Help Line will be made available to Users who seek help or their immediate relatives. Update of this service will be provided in the Application.
