How to Play the Small Town Lottery (STL) PARES?

  • First make sure you have enough game credits to play. Game credits can be found in the upper left of the application.
  • When you have enough game credits, just tap the BET button in the very bottom center of the application.
  • The BET button will be replaced by the PLACE BET button when you are already on the STL game screen. Here you can choose a playable STL game: SWER2, SWER3 and PARES.
  • For the PARES game, first tap the first ball to select a number by tapping the numpad. Do this again for the second ball to produce two number combinations. Note that the balls have numbers from zero(0) to fourthy(40).
  • Next tap the vacant part on the side of the Straight or Ramble. You can then choose how much to bet on the combination you choose. Just tap the numpad to bet.
  • You can change the selected combination and bet on iteration in the second through fifth methods.
  • When you are sure of your chosen combinations and bets, simply tap the green PLACE BET button.
  • After that you will see all the combinations you have chosen and equal bets.
  • If you want to add a combination just tap the ball again and repeat the second to seventh procedure.
  • When you are satisfied with your bets, simply tap the PLACE BET button at the very bottom center of the application. A prompt will appear to confirm your action.
  • After that you will be given a ticket. The ticket will include Game Type, Draw Schedule, Ticket Date, Total Transactions, Total Amount and other important information. You will also receive the ticket via SMS.
  • Just repeat the previous procedures to be able to play SWER2, SWER3 and PARES.
  • For Game Rules go to Profile and look for Game Rule for more Information and details of the game you want to play.

HAPPY TO PLAY!
